Vegetarian Quinoa Salad

Vegetarian Quinoa Salad is a nutritious and flavorful dish featuring protein-rich quinoa, mixed vegetables, herbs, and a tangy vinaigrette dressing.

Cook quinoa and let it cool, then mix with cucumbers, tomatoes, bell peppers, and fresh herbs. Prepare a tangy dressing using lemon juice, olive oil, and spices. Toss the

ingredients together, ensuring they are well coated in the dressing. Serve chilled for a refreshing and flavorful vegetarian meal that is rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins


This are the ingredients of making Vegetarian Quinoa Salad

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

The following are the steps of making Vegetarian Quinoa Salad

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese on top (if using).
  5. Serve the quinoa salad chilled or at room temperature as a healthy and refreshing side dish.
