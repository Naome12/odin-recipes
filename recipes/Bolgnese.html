Classic Spaghetti Bolognese


Ingredients used to make Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps of preparing Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti and enjoy!
